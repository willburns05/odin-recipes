Lela's Protein Mango Smoothie

Description

Lela's Protein Mango Smoothie is a refreshing and nutrient-packed drink, perfect for a quick breakfast or post-workout boost. This smoothie combines the tropical sweetness of ripe mango with the creamy texture of Greek yogurt, creating a delightful balance of flavors. A scoop of vanilla protein powder enhances its nutritional profile, making it a filling and protein-rich option to keep you energized.

Blended with ice and a splash of milk, this smoothie achieves a perfectly smooth and chilled consistency. You can also customize it with optional add-ins like flaxseeds or spinach for extra fiber and nutrients. Whether you're on the go or simply craving something healthy and delicious, Lela's Protein Mango Smoothie is an easy-to-make treat that satisfies your taste buds and supports your wellness goals.

  • ½ mango, chopped, or more to taste
  • ½ cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ cup ice
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon honey, or to taste (Optional)
  1. Step 1
    Blend mango, yogurt, almond milk, ice, protein powder, and honey together in a blender until smooth.
